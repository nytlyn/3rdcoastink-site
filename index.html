ABOUT

3rd Coast Ink is a locally owned tattoo studio in Boise dedicated to high quality custom tattoo work and professional service. Our artists specialize in a wide range of styles and work closely with every client to create unique pieces that are built to last. Whether you're coming in for your first tattoo or adding to an existing collection, our goal is to provide a comfortable, welcoming environment where creativity and craftsmanship come first.
